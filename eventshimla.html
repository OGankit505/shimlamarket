EVENTS

Shimla Summer Festival

This is one of the most anticipated events in Shimla, held annually during the months of May or June. It celebrates the arrival of summer and includes cultural performances, music concerts, food stalls, and various competitions.

Ice Skating Carnival

Held in the month of December, this carnival celebrates the winter season in Shimla. The highlight of the event is ice skating on the natural ice rink at the Shimla Ice Skating Club.

Halda Festival

Celebrated by the Gaddi community in Shimla, the Halda Festival is an ancient tradition that marks the arrival of winter. It involves the lighting of torches and bonfires, and is believed to ward off evil spirits.

Sipi Fair

Held in Kinnaur, which is in close proximity to Shimla, the Sipi Fair is an important cultural event for the people of the region. It features traditional dances, music, and a variety of local products.
