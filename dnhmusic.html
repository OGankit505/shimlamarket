MUSIC

Tarpa Music

Tarpa is a traditional wind instrument made of bamboo, commonly used in tribal communities of Dadra and Nagar Haveli. It is played during festive occasions and celebrations.

Dholki and Dhol

These are percussion instruments used in traditional music and dance performances. The Dholki is a smaller drum, while the Dhol is a larger, double-headed drum.

Bhavai Music

Bhavai is a traditional folk theatre form that incorporates music, dance, and drama. The music used in Bhavai performances is an essential component of this art form.

Mand and Garba Music

While Garba and Dandiya Raas are more commonly associated with the neighboring state of Gujarat, they are also popular in Dadra and Nagar Haveli and Daman and Diu, especially during Navratri and other festive occasions.
