MARKET

Lakkar Bazaar

Lakkar Bazaar is known for its wooden crafts and souvenirs. You can find a variety of handmade items like walking sticks, wooden toys, and decorative pieces.

Mall Road

Mall Road is one of the most popular shopping destinations in Shimla. It offers a wide range of shops selling clothing, accessories, handicrafts, and souvenirs. It's a great place for leisurely strolls and shopping.

Bhuttico

Bhuttico is a cooperative society known for its hand-woven woollen products. It's a great place to buy high-quality shawls, stoles, and other woollen items.

Himachal Emporium

Himachal Emporium offers a wide range of traditional handicrafts and handloom products from the state of Himachal Pradesh. It's a good place to explore the local craftsmanship.
